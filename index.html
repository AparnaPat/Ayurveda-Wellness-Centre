Services

PanchaKarma

Panchakarma is an integral part of Ayurveda which helps achieve a balanced state of body, mind and consciousness through detoxification and rejuvenation.

Panchakarma is a set of five therapies designed mainly for cleansing the body of toxins. Ayurveda considers that the purification of the body is important before the commencement of any other therapy. The Five Therapies are Vaman,Virechan,Basti,Nasya and Raktamokshan.

Shirodhara

This is a specialized therapy aimed to relieve ailments related to the head region. In this therapy, a stream of warm oil is poured on the centre of the forehead through a vessel.

The temperature and speed of the oil stream are kept constant during the therapy that goes on for 30 to 60 minutes.

Abhyanga

Abhyanga is a type of body massage done using an appropriate herbal or medicated oil. It may be done for the entire body or a specific part such as the head, feet or joint etc.

Abhyanga ("oil massage") is a form of Ayurvedic medicine that involves massage of the body with dosha-specific warm herb infused oils. Proponents claim Abhyanga aids joint health, nourishes the dhatus (tissues of the body) and brings aggravated doshas back to balance. Its main purpose is to lubricate the body parts and pacify vata dosha.

Naysa

Nasya is a simple procedure where 3-5 drops of an herbalized oil, usually seasame or olive oil, is applied into the nostrils and sniffed.

This brings about the cleaning of the entire head region and relieves various types of headache, migraine, hair problems, sleep disorders, neurological disorders, sinusitis, chronic rhinitis and respiratory diseases.

Basti

Basti refers to the Enema therapy. Ayurvedic Basti involves injecting herbal substances like oil and decoction in a liquid medium into the intestine.

The therapy has enormous benefits, especially in complicated and chronic diseases. This therapy is extremely effective against vata-dominated conditions such as arthritis, piles and constipation.

Vaman

Vaman means therapeutic vomiting which is a medicated emesis. This treatment is done for the aggravated kapha dosha.

Once the toxins get liquefied and gather in upper cavities of body, the patient is given emetic medicine and decoction. Vaman therapy is especially recommended primarily for kapha-dominated conditions such as obesity, asthma and hyperacidity.
